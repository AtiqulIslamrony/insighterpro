# Privacy Policy for Insighter Pro

**Last Updated: January 15, 2026**

## Overview

Insighter Pro ("we", "our", or "the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our Chrome extension.

## Data Collection

### What We DO NOT Collect
- ❌ Personal identification information
- ❌ Browsing history
- ❌ Passwords or login credentials
- ❌ Financial information
- ❌ Email addresses
- ❌ Any data from websites you visit

### What We DO Collect
- ✅ **Nothing.** All analysis happens locally in your browser.

## How the Extension Works

Insighter Pro operates entirely within your browser:

1. **Local Analysis Only**: All tracking pixel detection, dataLayer inspection, and technology detection happens locally on your device.

2. **No External Servers**: We do not transmit any data to external servers.

3. **No Analytics**: We do not use any analytics or tracking within the extension itself.

4. **No Cookies**: The extension does not set any cookies for tracking purposes.

## Permissions Explained

| Permission | Purpose |
|------------|---------|
| `activeTab` | Required to analyze the current webpage |
| `scripting` | Required to inject detection scripts into pages |
| `storage` | Used only to save your preferences (dark/light mode) |
| `tabs` | Required to get current tab information |
| `cookies` | Required to display cookies in the Storage Manager (read-only analysis) |
| `webNavigation` | Required to detect page navigation for real-time updates |
| `webRequest` | Required to monitor network requests for pixel detection |
| `<all_urls>` | Required to work on any website you choose to analyze |

## Data Storage

The only data stored locally on your device:
- Your theme preference (dark/light mode)
- Extension settings

This data never leaves your browser and can be cleared at any time through Chrome's extension settings.

## Third-Party Services

Insighter Pro does not integrate with or send data to any third-party services.

## Children's Privacy

Our extension does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13 years of age.

## Changes to This Policy
