üìñ Our Story

Insighter Pro was born from a simple frustration: debugging marketing pixels shouldn't be painful.

As digital marketers and developers, we spent countless hours inspecting network requests, digging through dataLayer arrays, and manually checking if tracking was firing correctly. Every existing tool felt either too limited or too complex.

So we built something better.

This extension is the result of collaboration between human creativity and AI-assisted development. Every feature was carefully designed, tested, and refined to provide the best debugging experience possible.

Today, Insighter Pro helps marketers and developers across the globe debug GA4, Meta Pixel, TikTok, Google Ads, and 10+ tracking platforms ‚Äî all from one beautiful interface.
